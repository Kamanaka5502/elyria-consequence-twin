# Elyria Consequence Twin Scan

## Know where action becomes consequence before it binds.

Elyria Consequence Twin Scan is a 7–10 day operational diagnostic that maps where AI, workflow, access, approval, payment, deployment, or customer-operation actions may bind consequence without sufficient authority, evidence, custody, standing, or replay.

## What It Finds

- consequence paths that can become real without valid admission
- authority assumptions and expired standing
- evidence gaps before operational effect
- custody breaks across teams, systems, or vendors
- AI/workflow outputs influencing protected outcomes
- undefined refusal conditions
- missing revalidation triggers
- replay gaps that prevent later proof
