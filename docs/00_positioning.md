# Positioning

## Category

**Elyria Consequence Twin** is a digital twin for consequence-bearing execution.

It is not an org chart, process map, GRC dashboard, risk register, approval log, or generic AI governance layer.

It maps where action can become real, what authority admits it, what evidence supports it, where standing expires, what must be refused, what must be revalidated, and what replay will prove later.

## Core Promise

Elyria Consequence Twin tells an organization where execution can bind, where it must not bind, and what proof must exist before action becomes operational reality.

## Short Lines

- A digital twin for consequence-bearing execution.
- Know where action becomes consequence before it binds.
- Map where execution can bind before it becomes operational reality.
- Find the black paths: consequence movement with no lawful or provable admission.

## Market Gap

Most systems answer:

```text
Who approved this?
What is the process?
What risks exist?
What controls are mapped?
What happened in the log?
```

Elyria answers:

```text
Can this consequence legally, operationally, evidentially, and structurally become real right now?
```

That distinction is the product category.
